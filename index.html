About Me

I am a Research Specialist at the Alliance of Bioversity International and CIAT, working at the intersection of remote sensing, crop modeling, and artificial intelligence.

I develop data-driven tools that transform satellite and climate data into actionable insights for agriculture.

With a PhD from the University of Tokyo and an MPhil in Astronomy and Astrophysics from the University of Manchester, my work bridges advanced geospatial analytics with real-world agricultural challenges.
