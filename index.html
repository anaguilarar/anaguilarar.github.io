Bringing Digital Tools to Agriculture

Remote Sensing and Machine Learning

Andrés Aguilar Ariza

I am a Research Specialist at the Alliance of Bioversity International and CIAT (October 2024), where I implement crop modeling and remote sensing approaches to support data-driven decision-making in agriculture.

I hold a PhD degree from the University of Tokyo, where my research focused on advancing plant phenotyping through the integration of remote sensing and machine learning techniques.

I completed an MPhil in Astronomy and Astrophysics at the University of Manchester, where I applied machine learning and big data techniques to satellite imagery for rice phenology monitoring.

My background as an Agricultural Engineer, combined with experience in crop modeling, geospatial analysis, and machine learning approaches, centers on leveraging Earth observation and data science to improve agricultural systems. I am passionate about bridging advanced geospatial analytics with real-world farming challenges.
