Bringing Digital Tools to Agriculture

Remote Sensing and Machine Learning

Andrés Aguilar Ariza

I am currently based in Tokyo, finishing my PhD studies at the University of Tokyo. My work is focused on plant phenotyping at the Plant Nutrition and Fertilizer laboratory. My research aims to speed up plant phenotyping by implementing remote sensing and machine learning techniques. I completed my Master's degree at the University of Manchester where I attempted to detect rice fields using satellite data .
